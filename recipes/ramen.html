Ramen

Description

Ramen is a great dish. I usually just like mine with noodles, broth, chicken, and light vegetables though.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken
  • Your favorite vegetable

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil then put your ramen noodles in. At the same time, start to cook your chicken in a pan.
  2. Once the noodles are soft and the chicken is fully cooked, put the noodles in a bowl and cut up chicken then put it on top.
  3. I like to steam broccoli so I would start that now in the microwave. After about 3 minutes or so it should be fully cooked.
  4. Add the vegetables on top.
  5. Enjoy your ramen noodles.
