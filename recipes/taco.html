Taco

Description

The steak street taco is one of the best foods in the world. My favorite place to get them from is Guerrerros and thankfully it's only a block away from my house!! Steak street tacos pretty simple in that they only have meat, veggies, and a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Steak
  • Onion
  • Celantro
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan or skillet to medium heat then add the steak to the pan and cook it for 2-3 minutes each side or until browned.
  2. Warm the tortillas in the same skillet and remove to your liking.
  3. Chop the steak, onion, celantro, and lime.
  4. Add the steak, onion, and celantro on top of the tortilla.
  5. Squeeze lime juice from the lime slice on top of your taco.
  6. Enjoy the taco.
