Pierogi

Description

As an American with a Polish heritage, my family makes pierogi every winter and it's become one of my favorite foods. It's basically just a dumpling and can be filled with anything you can think of. My favorite are potato though.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Butter
  • Farmers Cheese
  • Mashed Potatoes

Steps

  1. Create a cirle of dough about 6 inches in diameter.
  2. Use a tablespoon to fill the dough with a mixture of farmers cheese and mashed potatoes. Make sure not to overfill the dough or it will open in future steps.
  3. Next, fold the dough around the filling and use a fork to press down the edges and create a riffle along the edge.
  4. Boil the pierogi for about 25 minutes. Hopefully it doesn't open as long as it's not too full.
  5. After boiling, let them sit for 20 minutes to drain then you can fry them in a pan until golden brown.
  6. Enjoy your pierogi and save one for me.
